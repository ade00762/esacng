    G-Five Business Accelerator Program

    I recently participated in the G-Five business accelerator program, delivered in partnership with the Lagos Business School Sustainability Centre. This transformative experience provided young female innovators and entrepreneurs with a deep understanding of key business concepts and strategies, particularly in Business Development and Climate Innovation. The program covered essential topics such as Sustainable Business Leadership, Legal Requirements and Financial Management, Brand Positioning and Digital Marketing, and Vision Boarding sessions.

    The Sustainable Business Leadership module taught us to integrate environmental and social responsibility into our business models, promoting long-term growth and societal contributions. Legal and financial insights were provided to help us navigate regulatory frameworks and ensure sound financial practices. Additionally, we learned effective brand positioning and digital marketing strategies to strengthen our online presence and engage target audiences.

    The Vision Boarding sessions were particularly inspiring, helping us visualize our goals and set clear, actionable objectives. The program was a valuable platform for connecting and sharing ideas with other young female entrepreneurs. The partnership with Lagos Business School Sustainability Centre ensured top-notch education and support, making this experience exceptionally beneficial for our entrepreneurial journeys. Future success.
